Chainsmokers

The Chainsmokers are an Electronic DJ duo consisting of Andrew Taggart, Alex Pall and Matt Mcguire

The Chainsmokers

Electronic DJ duo

The group has had twenty-four singles in several countries with the 2014 review song "#Selfie", reaching number sixteen on the US Billboard Hot 100, number three in Australia, and number eleven in the UK. They released their debut EP, Bouquet, in October 2015. The follow-up single "Roses" reached the top ten on the Billboard Hot 100, [1] while "Don't Let Me Down" became their first top five single and Closer , featuring Halsey , became their first number one single on Billboard.
